About

DevFest is the annual hackathon organized by BITS Pilani, Goa Campus. Our mission is to bring the community together to create innovative solutions to novel problems. We aspire to create a platform to bring together top minds with the focus on upcoming new technologies. So come join us and make most out of the current situation!

A world of devices connected by internet opens up opportunities for us that were previously unthinkable. Innovative apps can be built that can allow devices to share data and take intelligent decisions.
